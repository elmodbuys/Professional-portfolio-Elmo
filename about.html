About Me

A developer who understands how business actually works.

I'm Elmo - a Cape Town based frontend developer with a background that doesn't follow the usual route. Before I wrote code professionally, I spent nearly a decade in retail and the mobile industry, working directly with customers and businesses every single day.

That time taught me something you really can't get from a course: that a website has to work for the person behind it. It's not just a design exercise. It's a business tool - and it should feel like one.

I'm careful with my work. I take my time, I pay attention to the details, and I don't hand something over until I'm genuinely happy with it. I communicate clearly throughout every project, so there are never any surprises on your end
